How To attract Butterflies

Butterflies are one of the most beautiful types of insects in the world, and are the second largest group of insect other than beetles. If you are wondering how to attract them, This article while help you have a flourishing garden with tons of Butterflies!

Flowers

There are so many different Flowers to choose from, But here are my best bets..

    • Zinnia
      Bachelors Buttons/Coneflower
      Verbena bonariensis
      Butterfly Bush (sometimes invasive, check local restrictions or buy a seedless variety such as 'Blue Chip' )
      Yarrow
      Phlox
      Sunflower (Host plants for checkerspots)
      Joe pye weed
      Liatris
      Agastache
      Snapdragon(Host plant for many)
      Daisy
      Goldenrod(No worries, this plant does not cause allergies)
      Lantana
      Salvia
      Aster
      Coneflower
      Butterflyweed(Host for monarchs and Hairstreaks, not weedy as the name suggests)

    • Water

    Butterflies need water too! add a puddler to your yard to attract males with the nutrients it provides!

    DIY Puddler

    You will need....

    • A shallow plate, bowl, or tray
      Sand or compost
      water
    How to assemble
      Find a Sunny area protected from strong wind
          but the sand or compost to fill the plate, bowl, or tray
              Moisten the sand or compost till damp but not wet
                  Place in the sunny area and watch and wait for the Butterflies!
